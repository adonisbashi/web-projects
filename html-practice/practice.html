Interested in making your own pizza at Papa's? Follow the steps below!

  1. Decide whether you want round or square crust
  2. Choose your size
  3. Place the ring around the pizza and begin docking it to remove bubbles
  4. Sauce the pizza and put one cup of cheese
  5. Choose your toppings
    • Always put the meats before the veggies!
    • If theres only veggies, always put mushrooms first!
  6. Place on the left side of the oven if its a square or the right side if its a round.
  7. Cut and enjoy!
